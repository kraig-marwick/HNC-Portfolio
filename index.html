Portfolio Introduction

This portfolio has been created as part of the HNC Music graded unit and is designed to demonstrate my skills as a composer, performer and music producer.

The role of a composer involves creating and developing original musical ideas, applying knowledge of structure, harmony and arrangement, and using recording and production techniques to realise those ideas in a finished form.

This portfolio demonstrates these skills through original compositions, recording, performance, and supporting documentation.

About

I have always been a huge fan of music and remain fascinated by its ability to move people and evoke a wide range of emotions.

I have an eclectic taste in music and enjoy experimenting with different styles and instruments as part of my development.

I focus on guitar-led songwriting and structured arrangement, developing ideas into full recordings.

I have developed skills in songwriting, recording, performance and music theory using Studio One.

This portfolio documents my creative and technical development.
